LAT PULL-DOWNS

Step 1:Sitting on a bench,grab a bar with a false overhead thumb on the same side as your fingers shoulder-width grip.
Step 2:As you pull your shoulder blades together and down,while sticking your Chest out,pull the bar to your chest.Then,pause with the bar an inch or so from your chest,and slowly let it raise to where it began.Throughout,keep your chest out.
