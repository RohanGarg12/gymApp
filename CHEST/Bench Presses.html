Bench Presses

Step 1:Lay flat on a bench and have your feet flat on the floor.The bar,when racked,will be slightly behind your head.If you are using a free bar ,to eliminate the chance of dropping the weight on yourself.
Step 2:Once you have lifted the weight off of the rack,the bar will be directly over the center of your chest.press the bar back up to an extended position,focusing on driving the ar upwards with your chest and arms.
